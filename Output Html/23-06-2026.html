Shri Ambabai Temple Development Project Launched in Kolhapur

Source: TH

Union Home Minister laid the foundation for the new Pradakshina Marg and conservation work of the Parivar Devata Mandirs at the famous Ambabai Mahalaxmi temple in Kolhapur.

  • About: The project is being developed under the idea of “Vikas Bhi, Virasat Bhi”, combining pilgrimage infrastructure with heritage conservation. It involves the renovation and corridor development of the historic Shri Ambabai Mahalaxmi Temple at an estimated cost of around ₹1,500 crore.
  • Heritage Conservation: Special emphasis is being placed on preserving the temple’s historical and cultural character, including the conservation of 41 smaller temples around the main shrine in consultation with archaeological authorities.
  • Modern Pilgrimage Facilities: The project will provide facilities such as a Darshan Mandap, Annachhatra, multipurpose hall, heritage galleries, light-and-sound show, AI-based security systems, LED lighting, parking and first-aid facilities.
  • Tourism and Local Economy: The project aims to transform Kolhapur into a major spiritual tourism destination, improve the devotee experience, generate employment and boost the local economy through tourism-led development.

Ambabai Mahalaxmi Temple

  • About: Located in the sacred Karveer region, it is one of India’s revered Shakti Peethas and has been a major centre of faith for devotees for centuries.
  • Presiding Deity: It is dedicated to Goddess Lakshmi , locally worshipped as Ambabai , the goddess of wealth and prosperity.
  • Architecture and Idol: The temple is known for its Hemadpanti architectural style and houses a black stone idol of Goddess Lakshmi , adorned with jewels and flowers.
  • Temple Complex: The complex also includes shrines dedicated to Lord Vishnu, Lord Shiva, Lord Ganesha and other deities , making it a major cultural and spiritual centre of Kolhapur.
